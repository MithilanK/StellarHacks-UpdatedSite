About

What is Stellar Hacks?

Working with the Child Rights and You Foundation (Cry), Stellar Hacks strives to enhance the lives of underprivileged children in India through the power of coding. We aim to unite the bright coders and thinkers of our future generation while also helping children in rural areas attain the resources to achieve their full potential.

How Does it Work?

Stellar Hacks is an in-person Hackathon Competition where students from the Bay Area come together and create projects. There is a given theme and contestants compete to create the best project that follows the theme. People can go into teams of up to 6 people and create projects.

Where is it?

Stellar Hacks is hosted at the MPR at Foothill Highschool

Who Runs it?

The Stellar Hacks Team consists of Foothill Highschool Students.
